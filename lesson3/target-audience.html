The target audience will be everyone. Weather affects everyone and everybody benefits from the effective utilization of reliable weather services.

Scenario 1:

A couple college students wants to go hiking. They want to know what the weather will be like to see when would be the best time to go. This website will provide the information of when the best time would be.

Scenario 2:

An adventurer. Traveling, going on a road trip. She would want to check this website to see if she should go out on an adventurer and go for a drive or if she should stay in the house and do it another day.

Scenario 3:

A college student would check this website to see which outfit she would choose for the day. Yeah, she's one of those college girls who wants and needs to look cute for boys.

Scenario 4:

A young adult, thinking of going on a sledding date. He would check this website to see how cold it would be outside and see if his date is still down to go on this date and if she would enjoy the freezing temperature. Or maybe they can just get hot cocoa and keep themselves warm.

Scenario 5:

A group of friends wanting to play some outdoor basketball would want to check this website and see how hot it would be outside. They will not only see the temperature but also see how humid it would be outside. They might reconsider playing basketball indoors then.
